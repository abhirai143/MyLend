Introduction.

MYLEND is a Loan and Debt Management Udhar Ledger , Account Book for a lender to manage their private loans or credit debit with friends, family, and colleagues. Replace your traditional payment reminder book or khata book with new digital ledger debt management software. In addition to its Loan Management capabilities, MYLEND includes many other useful tools, such as interest calculator and a recurring deposit tracker. With MYLEND, you have everything you need to take control of your finances and achieve your financial goals. This Digital Interest ledger allows users to do small business accounting from adding customers, creating or deleting loans, recording transactions, Automated SMS notifications on loan creation and payments , Transaction Reports.

Who We Are ?

Our goal is to Bridge the Credit Gap for Bharat through Lenders. We provide solutions to improve credit & finance, making it simplest and fastest for Bharat. Money lenders extend loans from their business revenue and wealth or savings, they also rely on loans from other informal lenders for lending capital. Our Aim is to Simplify their Lending Business and help them extend credit to more People who don't have access to Formal Credit. managing their Debt and Savings.
